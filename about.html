ABOUT US

EUFi is the abbreviation for EUDOKIA FILM INDUSTRY. EUDOKIA is a Greek word meaning good pleasure, well pleased and the best that's why our Motto is "the best in creativity" .It was established in 2018 by LordPhilips Tasie (GEPD)who has served the Nigerian Nollywood movie industry in various notable capacities and has taken part in various movie as a crew and actor some of which includes shattered dreams, covenant of our forefathers, emperor the general,in the land of okpuafor,ijiji Na achakata etc. We are not just focused on film production,we help people of all ages discover, develop, utilise and turn their talents (acting, music, dancing, modelling, graphic designing, website design and development etc)into wealth.
